What the patron experiences

A fast, private, and accessible self-service experience that respects the diversity of your gaming floor. Patrons select their language at the start of every transaction — not as a buried setting. Amount presets are configurable per property. Responsible gaming limits are enforced transparently before amount selection, not as an afterthought.

  • Language selection at start of every session
  • Configurable amount presets per property
  • Spending limit enforcement before amount selection
  • Province-specific receipt templates
  • Self-exclusion checked at transaction start

What the cashier experiences

A cage workstation built around the pace of your busiest night. Pending transactions appear the moment they are authorized at the kiosk. Cashiers search by card last four, not by transaction ID or time of day. Every action — ID scan, signature, disbursement, receipt print — happens on one screen without mode switching or nested navigation.

  • Pending queue with live authorization countdowns
  • Card last-four search — no transaction ID required
  • Single-scan OPOS ID verification
  • Void and reprint without calling support
  • Supervisor override and manager controls built in
