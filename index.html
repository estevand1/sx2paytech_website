The status quo

Canadian casinos have been paying too much for too long.

Traditional cash advance vendors earn a share of every transaction processed on your floor. The technology ages, contracts get longer, and revenue continues leaving the property.

01
You share every transaction

The buy rate model means your vendor earns a percentage of every dollar your patrons advance on the floor, including waived fees. That number compounds quietly and quickly.

02
The technology hasn't kept up

Legacy cash advance systems were not built for diverse payment needs.

03
The contract locks you in

Long terms with limited leverage at renewal. Feature requests that go unanswered. Price increases are absorbed because switching is complicated. The vendor relationship works better for the vendor than it does for you.

How It Works

Fast for Patrons. Accountable for Operators.

Every transaction is tracked, verified, and reconciled from authorization through settlement.

1
Patron Starts Transaction

The patron selects a language, chooses an amount, reviews fees, and completes a secure card authorization.

2
Transaction Authorized

The authorization is approved and instantly appears in the cage workstation.

3
Cashier Verifies Identity

The cashier retrieves the transaction, verifies identification, captures a signature, and disburses funds.

4
Funds Settle Directly to Casino Bank Account

Transactions settle directly to your merchant account, so you no longer have to wait for delayed settlements. SX2 provides the software and reporting layer that supports the operation.
