The process

Tracked from authorization through settlement.

Every cash advance transaction is tracked, verified, and reconciled from authorization through settlement.

1

Patron Starts Transaction

The patron selects a language, chooses an amount, reviews fees, and completes a secure card authorization. Responsible gaming limits are checked before amount selection.

2

Transaction Authorized

The authorization is approved and instantly appears in the cage workstation, with a live expiry timer.

3

Cashier Verifies Identity

The cashier retrieves the transaction, verifies government-issued identification, captures a digital signature, and disburses funds.

4

Funds Settle Directly to Your Merchant Account

Transactions settle directly to your casino's merchant account. SX2 provides the software and reporting layer. The settlement revenue belongs to the property.

Common questions

What gaming operators ask before switching.

What is the SX2 PayTech pricing model? add

SX2 operates on a flat monthly licence fee model. You pay a fixed monthly fee for the software platform and keep all of the transaction revenue your patrons generate on the floor. There are no per-transaction fees and no buy-rate percentages paid to SX2. Pricing is based on property configuration. Contact us for a quote specific to your operation.

Which markets does SX2 PayTech serve? add

SX2 works with licensed gaming operators across the United States and Canada. In Canada, this includes properties regulated by the British Columbia Lottery Corporation (BCLC), the Alberta Gaming, Liquor and Cannabis commission (AGLC), and Ontario. In the United States, SX2 supports operators licensed under applicable state gaming commissions. The platform is built to support compliance requirements, reporting obligations, and responsible gaming standards for each jurisdiction.

How long does implementation take? add

Most properties are live within 30 days. SX2 introduces the relationship with the gateway and acquirer and ties into them as part of the implementation. The process includes system configuration, cage staff training, and a supervised go-live period. SX2 provides onboarding support throughout. Your gaming licence and existing regulatory relationships are not affected by the switch.

Is SX2 PayTech compliant with gaming regulations in the US and Canada? add

Yes. The platform is PCI DSS Level 1 certified and P2PE encrypted. In Canada, SX2 is PIPEDA compliant and includes FINTRAC AML reporting with responsible gaming controls for BCLC, AGLC, Ontario, and other provincial requirements. In the United States, SX2 supports BSA/AML reporting obligations and responsible gaming controls for state-licensed operators. Contact us for jurisdiction-specific compliance documentation.

What languages does the patron kiosk support? add

The SX2 patron kiosk supports six languages: English, French, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Punjabi, and Arabic. Arabic includes full right-to-left layout and correctly rendered typography, not a transliteration overlay. Language selection is available at the start of every transaction.

What happens if the system goes down? add

SX2 operates at 99.99% uptime on Google Cloud infrastructure across North America. For questions about support availability, SLA terms, and incident response for gaming floors, contact our team directly. Response and escalation details are provided to all licensed operators during onboarding.
